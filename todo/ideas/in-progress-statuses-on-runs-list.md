# In-Progress Statuses on Runs List

## What
Show live in-progress statuses for runs directly in the runs list.

## Why
Users currently have no visibility into actively running pipelines without navigating away — surfacing live status reduces context switching and makes the runs list the single source of truth.

## Who
Engineers monitoring active CI runs.

## Rough appetite
medium

## Notes
The hard part is syncing updates as they come — requires a real-time or polling mechanism to keep statuses fresh without full-page refreshes.
