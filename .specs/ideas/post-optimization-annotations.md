# Post-Optimization Annotations

## What
Allow users to annotate jobs and tests after optimization with the date, model type, and a note.

## Why
Provides traceability for optimization decisions — makes it easy to see when something was tuned, what model was used, and why, without digging through git history or external docs.

## Who
Engineers reviewing CI performance or auditing past optimization decisions.

## Rough appetite
unknown

## Notes
Annotation fields: date, model type, free-text note. Scope covers both jobs and tests.
